FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Why is everything email-only?

Email creates complete documentation. Every conversation, promise, and decision exists in writing you can reference forever. No forgotten details or misunderstandings.

It also respects your time. No scheduling conflicts or time zone issues. Read and respond when convenient for you. Everything is clear, accountable, and professional.

How much do your services cost?

Pricing varies based on campaign scope, duration, and promotional channels. Every campaign is customized to your specific book and goals.

Submit your book information through my form. Within 48 hours, you'll receive a detailed custom strategy including specific pricing. No commitment required.

Do you guarantee bestseller status?

No. Anyone guaranteeing bestseller status is lying. Too many factors are outside anyone's control: market conditions, competition, algorithms, book quality, cover, reviews, pricing, and more.

What I DO guarantee: professional execution of all promised activities, reaching thousands of targeted readers, complete transparency, and giving your book the best possible chance to succeed.

What if my book has few reviews?

I can run campaigns for books with few reviews, but review count affects results. Books with 10+ reviews typically perform significantly better.

If you have fewer than 5 reviews, I may recommend building reviews first before investing in major promotion. I'll be honest about what's realistic for your situation.

How quickly will I receive my custom plan?

Within 24 to 48 hours of receiving your complete book information. Most submissions receive responses within 24 hours.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes. If I fail to execute any promised promotional activity, I'll either complete it immediately or refund the proportional amount.

If your campaign hasn't started and you need to cancel, you receive a full refund minus any work already completed.

Can you help with series promotions?

Absolutely! Series promotions often involve promoting Book 1 heavily to drive read-through to later books. I can create coordinated strategies across multiple books.

Email me with your series details and I'll recommend the optimal approach for your situation.

Do you work with international authors?

Yes! Email communication makes international collaboration seamless. No time zone scheduling issues.

I primarily target English-speaking reader markets (US, UK, Canada, Australia). For Custom Campaigns, we can discuss specific international market targeting.
